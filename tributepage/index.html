About Sundar Pichai

Sundar Pichai is an Indian-American business executive and the CEO of Alphabet Inc. He was born on July 12, 1972, in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India. Pichai joined Google in 2004 and worked on various projects including Google Chrome and the Chrome OS. In 2015, he became the CEO of Google, and after the corporate restructuring in 2019, he also became the CEO of Alphabet Inc.

Pichai is known for his leadership and vision, guiding Google through major developments and acquisitions. Under his leadership, Google expanded its product offerings and has become one of the most valuable and influential companies in the world.

Key Achievements

  • Leading the development of Google Chrome, one of the most popular web browsers in the world.
  • Overseeing the successful launch of Android, the most widely used mobile operating system.
  • Expanding Google's product portfolio with innovative technologies such as Google Assistant, Google Home, and Google Pixel.
  • Driving the growth of Google's cloud computing services and AI initiatives.
